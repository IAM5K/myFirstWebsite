ENTRY REQUIREMENTS

The Cyber Security & Digital Forensics Division at VIT Bhopal University is one of the most active team of learners and experts in Central India.
Having a close association with Government Agencies and IT Industries, the division provides universally accepted and highly valuable
graduate and post-graduate engineering courses on Cyber Security & Digital Forensics.

B.Tech.
(CSE: Cyber Security & Digital Forensics)

An aggregate of 60% in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics in the qualifying examination (+2/Intermediate) with valid VITEEE Score Card.

.

M.Tech.
(CSE: Cyber Security & Digital Forensics)

B.E/B.Tech. Degree in any branch / M.S / M.Sc. Computer Science or IT or Software Engineering/ MCA or any other equivalent degree from any recognized University with a minimum aggregate of 60% marks with valid VITREE Score Card.

Ph.D
(Computer Science And Engineering)

60% marks (or a CGPA of 6.5 out of 10) in the qualifying examination in Engineering/ Science and post-graduate degree from recognized University, with valid VITREE Score Card.
